About the Store

Fashion with
a soul and
a story.

Welcome to Urban Threads By P, a boldly curated online fashion destination run by a woman with an unshakeable eye for style and an even bigger heart for her community.

Every single item in this store has been personally selected by P herself. From factory exclusives with barely-there imperfections, to second-hand treasures with years of life still in them, each piece is chosen because it deserves a second chance, and so does your wardrobe.

Whether you're a man, a woman, a teen, or shopping for your little ones, whether you live in jeans and sneakers or dress to impress, there is something here for you.

"Great style shouldn't be a privilege. It should be for everyone."
